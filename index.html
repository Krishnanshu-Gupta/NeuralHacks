What is Neural Hacks?

Neural Hacks is the largest student-run AI hackathon in the world. We want to make it easy for anyone to dream big and to advocate for the importance of diversity, inclusion, accessibility, and representation in technology, we created a platform where individuals of all backgrounds come together to develop solutions that solve societyâ€™s biggest issues. So, what are you waiting for? Join us to create #YourNeuralHacks.

Together, we create solutions that are diverse, inclusive, and accessible.

The Neural Hacks Community

DubHacks prioritizes bringing together individuals from a diverse range of backgrounds and experiences. Despite our differences, our community is connected and united by common characteristics, values, and a passion for leveraging technology to inculcate change. We've illustrated characters that were designed with the intention of showcasing diverse personalities and appearances, as well as the way technology can be a catalyst for human interaction and collaboration. We believe the descriptions of these characters are representative of the values and traits exemplified by our attendees. Everything you need to create something revolutionary We want to inspire students to discover the world around them by empowering them to learn and create by providing an environment for them to explore and build with workshops, mentorship, and speakers.
