What is Neural Hacks?

Neural Hacks is the largest student-run AI hackathon in the world. We want to make it easy for anyone to dream big and to advocate for the importance of diversity, inclusion, accessibility, and representation in technology. We created a platform where individuals of all backgrounds come together to develop solutions that solve society’s biggest issues. So, what are you waiting for? Join us to create #YourNeuralHacks.

CONNECT

We connect technology with humanity.

EMPOWER

We empower students to innovate within their communities.

CREATE

Together, we create solutions that are diverse, inclusive, and accessible.

The Neural Hacks Community

NeuralHacks prioritizes bringing together a global community of people to raise interest in the field of Artificial Intelligence. With the expansion of Computer Science in the world in every single aspect, AI stands out for its possibilities and shaping the future of computer science. We bring this hackathon as a platform for the next big minds to give us a glimpse into what true creativity is with their masterpieces. We believe that no one should shape the future alone. We want to show that working with others is critical to success, and collaboration is a stepping stone to innovation. Inspire everyone.
